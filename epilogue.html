The Dewangan Editorial

Epilogue

A letter to you

“ The wisdom between the lines. ”

They say an archive is a way of keeping time still, but as I looked through these photographs and letters, I realized that for you, time never really stood still. It moved with purpose. You have always been in motion—learning, giving, and building a world for us that felt safe and full of possibility.

“A legacy is not what we leave behind, but what we set in motion in the hearts of others.”

Dad, you taught me that the best way to honor a memory is to live with the same curiosity and kindness that you do. This digital editorial is my small way of pressing our family's most cherished moments into pages that will never fade.

Thank you for the books we shared, the comfortable silences we sat in, and the wisdom you never had to speak aloud because you lived it every single day.

Happy Birthday, Dad. With all my love, always.

Shibu
